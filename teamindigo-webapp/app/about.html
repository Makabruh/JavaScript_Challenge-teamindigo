About PennyWise

Our Challenge:

“With the UK experiencing the biggest price hike for fifty years, we challenge you to build a product, app, or website that will empower everyday citizens to manage their budgets and help alleviate the challenges of the current cost of living crisis.”

Our Project:

Our project aims to help people manage their budgets by reviewing their monthly expenses and guiding them towards ways to reduce their expenses, for example using comparison websites for insurance or deciding if certain subscriptions are necessary etc.
